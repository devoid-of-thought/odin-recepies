Fast chickpeas

Ingredients

  • Can or jar of chickpeas
  • Two large onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Spices of choice (paprika, garlic powder, cumin, etc.)
  • Greek yoghurt or rice

Steps

  1. Drain and rinse the chickpeas.
  2. Chop the onions.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they are soft (or until they're caramelized).
  5. Add the chickpeas to the pan and stir well to combine with the onions.
  6. Sauté for a few minutes until the chickpeas are cooked through.
  7. Season with salt and your choice of spices. Stir well to ensure the chickpeas are evenly coated.
  8. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chickpeas are desired level of crispy.
  9. Serve hot with Greek yoghurt or over rice.
