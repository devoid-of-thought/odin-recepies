Gyoza

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 400 g flour
  • 200 ml hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (optional)

For the filling:

  • 500-800 g napa cabbage, finely chopped
  • 400-500 g lean ground meat
  • Scallions (green onions), chopped
  • A little soy sauce
  • A little sesame oil
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • A little fresh ginger, grated

Steps

  1. Pour hot water over the flour, add salt (and optional sesame oil), mix, and knead into a smooth dough.
  2. Cover and leave the dough to rest for 15-30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, place the finely chopped napa cabbage in a bowl, sprinkle with salt, and set aside for 15 minutes to let it release its water.
  4. Squeeze the excess water out of the cabbage thoroughly.
  5. Add the ground meat, chopped scallions, soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger to the drained cabbage. Mix everything well to complete the filling.
  6. Prepare the wrappers: you can either roll out the entire dough and cut out circles, or pinch off small pieces and roll them out individually (though this takes more time).
  7. Place a portion of the filling onto each wrapper, fold, and pinch the edges to seal the gyoza.
