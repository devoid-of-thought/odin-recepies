Loaded potato soup

Ingredients

  • 1,5-2kg of large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 5-10 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 100-200 g of bacon
  • 20 g butter
  • 3 tbsp of flour
  • 500 ml of milk
  • 1000 ml of vegetable/chicken broth or water + stock cube
  • 250ml of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chives or green onions for garnish
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, cooked bacon bits, sour cream

Steps

  1. In a large pot cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove and set aside, leaving the fat.
  2. Add the butter and onions. Saut√© until the onions are softened. Stir minced garlic and cook for another minute.
    3. \
  3. Add the flour and stir until smooth. Cook for 1-2 minutes
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk, followed by the broth. Bring to a simmer and add the potatoes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Add potatoes to the pot and bring to a boil. Cook until potatoes are fork tender
  7. Use a potato masher or immersion blender to blend the soup to your desired consistency.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chives or green onions and your choice of toppings.
